Why Dubai

Dubai a city with reputation for luxury and innovation

Visit Dubai for a unique blend of futuristic luxury, world-class shopping, and thrilling adventures, alongside rich cultural experiences in historic souks and desert landscapes, all within a remarkably safe, multicultural, and clean environment with sunshine year-round, offering everything from towering skyscrapers (Burj Khalifa) to unique desert safaris and vibrant global festivals.

Cafes to visit in Dbai

Arabian Tea House Restaurant & Cafe

Arabian Tea House is a renowned Emirati restaurant and an authentic Arabic food restaurant in Dubai, offering halal traditional arab cuisine.

Address

Al Fahidi Street, Bur Dubai, Bastakiya

Why it is so popular

The menu is a delightful mix of Arabic and Emirati flavors, prepared using fresh and locally-sourced ingredients. The decor is also an ode to Emirati culture, with Arabic-inspired interiors and cozy seating areas.

Learn more

Orto cafe

Orto Dubai is an outdoor garden-style cafe and restaurant hub in Dubai Design District, built in a converted industrial yard.

Address

Dubai Design District (d3),Building 6,Dubai, United Arab Emirates

Why it is so popular

It offers a rare, relaxed green escape from the city with multiple dining and bar options in one vibrant, communal space.

Learn more

TEN 11 (The Dubai Mall)

an upscale restaurant and lounge located at the prestigious Fashion Avenue extension of The Dubai Mall.It is a popular destination for special occasions, evening drinks, and high-end social dining.

Address

Level 2 Fashion Avenue The Dubai Mall - Dubai - United Arab Emirates

Why it is so popular

It is known for its fine dining experience, featuring a menu with Japanese and Peruvian influences, a sophisticated cocktail bar, and a luxurious, elegant atmosphere with views of the Burj Khalifa and the Dubai Fountain.
